A Note to Colin

Since 2023

Hello Colin. Honestly, writing this for you and the boys has been one of the hardest things I’ve done, because why does everything I write end up sounding so dramatic? But whatever, I’m sticking with it. I chose Eric Draven for you because it fits the way you carry yourself. You’ve always had that quiet, intense presence, hard to fully read, but impossible to ignore once you do. There’s a depth to you that most people don’t notice at first, but it’s definitely there. The one thing I’m sure about you is your loyalty, it runs deep, even if you don’t really talk about it. You’re the kind of person who sticks around in ways that actually count, and that’s something I respect more than I can properly explain. And yeah, you’re easily the best musician out of all of us. That’s just facts at this point. I know it’s not always easy being you. You carry a lot, more than you show, and most of it you deal with quietly. But you still keep going anyway, and I think that says more about your strength than anything else ever could.

Author: Jauza Rifa Abdurrafi
